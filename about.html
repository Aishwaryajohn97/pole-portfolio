About Me

Aishwarya John Pole

Hello! I'm Aishwarya, a self-driven and detail-oriented web and POS system developer based in Ilford, UK. I specialize in building visually appealing, functional, and responsive websites and software solutions that empower small businesses across the globe.

Over the last few years, I’ve helped businesses in the UK, Ghana, and beyond establish a strong online presence and streamline their operations through customized Point of Sale (POS) systems. Whether you're just getting started or looking to upgrade your current setup, I provide tailored solutions that are both cost-effective and impactful.

💼 My Mission

My mission is simple — to help entrepreneurs and small business owners bring their vision to life online, without the usual stress or high cost. I believe technology should be accessible, intuitive, and built around your real-world needs.

🛠 What I Can Build For You

  • Business Websites: Full-featured websites with services, contact forms, product pages, and mobile responsiveness.
  • Portfolio Sites: Clean and professional portfolios for creatives, freelancers, and individuals showcasing their work.
  • IT/Corporate Sites: Designed for companies offering technology services, with career pages, inquiry forms, and structured navigation.
  • POS Systems: Lightweight and effective sales systems that work locally or online — includes sales tracking, invoicing, and reporting.
  • Hosting Support: I help you get set up on platforms like GoDaddy, Vercel, or Namecheap, and make your site live with a custom domain.

📌 Why Clients Trust Me

  • ✔️ You work directly with me — I handle your project from start to finish with personal attention.
  • ✔️ I offer transparent pricing, no surprises, and flexible support options.
  • ✔️ My work is clean, reliable, and built to scale with your business needs.
  • ✔️ I prioritize simplicity and usability — both for you and your customers.
